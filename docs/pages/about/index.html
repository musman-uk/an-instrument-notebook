About Seasonal Notes

Seasonal Notes is a small, quiet experiment in noticing. Instead of building a traditional website with tidy pages and rigid structure, this project gathers fragments — small seasonal impressions — and scatters them across a single canvas.

The notes are intentionally messy, a little crooked, like sticky notes pinned to a wall or scribbles left on a desk. They aren’t meant to stand alone as pages. They belong together, side by side, forming a loose collage of the year.

This page exists only to explain the intention behind the form — a companion note to the main piece.
