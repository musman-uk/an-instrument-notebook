Seasonal Notes
Spring

The first warm breeze arrived today, carrying the scent of wet soil.

Summer

The air felt heavy today, thick with sunlight and the hum of insects.

Autumn

The leaves looked like they were giving up today,
letting go in slow spirals,
as if the air had finally convinced them
to stop pretending to hold on.

Winter

Frost on the window this morning —
thin, crooked lines like someone
scribbled on the glass while half‑asleep.
